Roasted Broccoli

Description

This oven roasted broccoli recipe is easy and versatile. The crisp, golden brown florets are delicious as a side dish or in all sorts of broccoli recipes!

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions below.


Ingredients

  • 14 ounces of broccoli;
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil;
  • ground black pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 °F (200 °C);
  2. Cut the broccoli florets from the stalk;
  3. Peel the stalk and slice into 1/4-inch slices;
  4. Mix the florets and stem pieces with olive oil in a bowl and transfer to a baking sheet.
  5. Season with salt and pepper;
  6. Roast in the preheated oven until the broccoli is tender and lightly browned; about 18 to 20 minutes.
