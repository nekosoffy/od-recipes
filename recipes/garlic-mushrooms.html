Garlic Mushrooms

Description

These garlic mushrooms are easy to make by sautéing sliced mushrooms in butter with red wine. Delicious with grilled steaks or on top of toasted bread for a tasty appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of butter;
  • 2 pounds of sliced fresh mushrooms;
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced;
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil;
  • 1 cup of red wine.

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a skillet over medium heat;
  2. Add the mushrooms and garlic;
  3. Cook and stir in basil until mushrooms are a light golden brown and the liquid has evaporated; about 10 minutes;
  4. Reduce the heat to low, and pour wine into the skillet;
  5. Season with salt and pepper;
  6. Simmer until wine has mostly evaporated. Serve immediately.
