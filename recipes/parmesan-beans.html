Parmesan Beans

Description

These flavorful steamed green beans are simply dressed for an easy side dish that practically cooks itself while you get the rest of the meal together.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh green beans, trimmed;
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil;
  • 3 tablespoons of freshly grated Parmesan cheese;
  • 2 tablespoons of white wine vinegar;
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley;
  • Salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water up to just below the bottom of the steamer;
  2. Bring water to a boil;
  3. Add the green beans, cover, and steam to your desired degree of tenderness; about 5 minutes;
  4. Transfer the steamed green beans to a serving bowl.
  5. Add olive oil, Parmesan cheese, white wine vinegar, garlic, salt, and pepper;
  6. Toss until evenly coated and let stand for 10 minutes;
  7. Remove the garlic slices and garnish with parsley before serving.
